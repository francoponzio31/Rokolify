With Rokolify, create a shared music experience using Spotify. Enable your Spotify player so your friends can add the music they want to hear to the playback queue.

How does it work?

  • Set up your account: First, link your Spotify account. Then, you can choose which songs your guests can add, either by selecting them from your playlists or by searching for any song in the Spotify catalog.
  • Invite your friends: Share the guest access link with your friends so they can join the fun.
  • Add songs: Each guest can add songs to your Spotify playback queue.
  • Enjoy together: Listen to the songs everyone chooses, creating a unique musical experience.

Key features:

  • Easy to use: Browse, search, and add Spotify songs without hassle.
  • Collaborative: Let your friends influence the music and keep the party going.
  • Integration with Spotify: All songs are played directly from your Spotify account, and you have access to its catalog.
  • Control access to your account: Once you decide to end the collaborative experience, you can delete the access link you generated so that guests can no longer add songs to your player.
